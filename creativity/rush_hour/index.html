Word Rush Hour

Rush hour where the cars are words.

In the following variation of rush hour, the cars are words. Any time a car finishes movement, the resulting grid must contain only valid words. Cars can only move forwards or backwards in the direction they are facing, and can move many spaces at once.

Word Rush Hour

Instructions:

  • Click a car to select it
  • Use the keyboard arrow keys to move selected car
  • Move the red car horizontally to the exit arrow
  • Moves count once per track selection
  • All consecutive letters must form valid words
  • The whole grid does not need to be connected
  • Individual letters can stand alone and move both forwards and backwards.
